Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to EatWell. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Delivery address and location details
  • Order history and payment details (no card numbers stored)
  • Device information (browser, IP address, OS)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your food orders
  • To improve our services and menus
  • To communicate about offers, promotions, or updates
  • To ensure secure payments and fraud prevention

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share it with:

  • Delivery partners to complete your order
  • Payment gateways for secure transactions
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, keep you logged in, and show relevant recommendations. You can disable cookies anytime from your browser settings.

6. Data Protection

We use encryption, secure servers, and strict protection methods to ensure your personal data stays safe and private.

7. Your Rights

  • Update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of promotional messages
